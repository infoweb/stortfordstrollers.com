Come and join the fun!

Walking basketball is a low cardio impact, active team sport enjoyed by both men and women together, covering all ages and abilities.

You don't have to be a Michael Jordon to enjoy a game. Many of our members last played basketball at school and some never at all! It's all about taking part, having a run (walk) around and getting some exercise in a friendly and social atmosphere.

Stortford Strollers was formed in January 2016 when a group of parents and footballing friends were looking for an alternative to being a lab rat on a gym treadmill but unable, due to age or injury, to take part in highly competitive sport. Since then numbers have grown but we always welcome new members.

If you can bounce and throw a ball you can join in!
